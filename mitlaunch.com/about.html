Who We Are

MIT Launch is a part of the Martin Trust Center for MIT Entrepreneurship. That means a few things:

MIT Standards

We follow MIT’s Standards of Excellence and Rigor in education, advising and practical experience. True to the motto of MIT, in all of our courses and throughout our activities, we operate on a hybrid model that fuses academic and practitioner perspectives - Mens et Manus. Plus, we are an honest broker: No one at MIT Launch will ever take a part of the new companies that we nurture and assist.

MIT Network

The Martin Trust Center for MIT Entrepreneurship works closely with other MIT departments, labs, centers and groups to connect students with the best entrepreneurship programming across the Institute. Plus, it hosts a family of programs that Launch is proud to be a part of.

MIT Growth

We will continually try new programs and activities to be able to have a bigger reach and impact through entrepreneurship education. If we fail, we learn in the process. When we succeed, we aim to scale rapidly. We are educators. Our goal is your success.
